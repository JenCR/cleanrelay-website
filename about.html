About Clean Relay

Cleaning, simplified
for DFW.

Clean Relay is a residential cleaning marketplace built right here in the DFW metro. We connect homeowners, property managers, and businesses with vetted, independent cleaning professionals so everyone gets what they need without the hassle.

Our Story

Built to solve a real problem.

Finding a reliable cleaner in DFW shouldn't be this hard. Between inconsistent no-shows, unclear pricing, and platforms that feel like they were built for someone else's city the whole experience was broken.

Clean Relay was built to fix that. A marketplace model that works for both sides: homeowners and businesses get a seamless, transparent booking experience, and cleaning professionals get consistent work, fair pay, and the flexibility to run their own schedule.

We're not a cleaning company. We're the platform that makes the whole thing work and we're built specifically for DFW.

"The market needed a platform that treated cleaning professionals like the skilled tradespeople they are and treated clients like they deserved a real experience."
— Jennifer, Founder & CEO
How We're Built

A marketplace, not a cleaning company.

Understanding our model matters especially if you're a business partner or a pro looking to join.

Client Books Online

Clients book directly through our platform clear pricing, no calls, no guesswork. Payment is collected securely at checkout.

We Match the Right Pro

Clean Relay routes the booking to a vetted independent cleaning professional in the right area with the right availability.

Pro Delivers the Clean

The assigned pro shows up, does the work, and handles it from start to finish. They bring their own supplies and equipment.

Everyone Gets Paid

Pros receive their payout weekly. Clean Relay retains a platform fee. Transparent, simple, and fair for everyone involved.

What We Stand For

The principles we
build everything on.

Transparency first.

No hidden fees. No bait-and-switch pricing. What you see when you book is what you pay. Every time.

We pay at the top of the market because pros who feel valued show up, do great work, and stick around. That's good for everyone.

We'd rather be the most reliable platform in DFW than the biggest one in three cities. Quality first, growth second.

Booking a clean should take 60 seconds. Running your portfolio of properties shouldn't require a phone call. We build for simplicity.
